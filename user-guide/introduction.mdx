# Why Tailwind?
Tailwind simplifies and speeds up your DeFi experience on Cosmos. With Tailwind,
navigating 50+ Cosmos chains feels like using Ethereum L1, but without the high fees.

## 1-click Experience

Tailwind users have powerful advantages over
traditional wallet users. We abstract away funds move and make 
cross-chain UX seamless and gas-optimized.

Instead of 5 or more clicks and page refreshes, you have just 1.

## Gas in any Token 
We know it can be frustrating to have to manage over 50+ gas tokens.
With our wallet, you pay gas in any token across 50+ IBC chains.
