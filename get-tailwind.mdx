---
title: 'Get TAILWIND'
description: 'Download the extension and get an account.'
---

<Tip>
  If you have another Cosmos wallet installed, 
  you should use a new Chrome profile to download TAILWIND.
  This will prevent any conflicts between the two wallets.
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the extension.">
    Download the [Chrome extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/dpnfollacokcbkeiidhplhjpafkbfacj).
    <span className="text-white">Click on the extension</span> after download to start onboarding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create or import mnemonic to begin onboarding."> 
    You should see this screen after clicking on the extension.
     <img
      className="block"
      src="/images/onboard.png"
      alt="Create or import"
    />
    Create or import mnemonic during onboarding flow. You can
    create a new account or import an existing account
    from another wallet (Keplr, Leap, MetaMask, etc.). 
    <img
      className="block"
      src="/images/import.png"
      alt="Create or import"
    />

    Your keys are encrypted and never leaves the extension.
    Do NOT share or lose your Secret Recovery Phrase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done!">
    <img 
      className="block"
      src="/images/social-links.png"
      alt="Dashboard"
    />
    Click on "Go to Dapp" to use our dashboard. Pin the extension for best experience. 

    You will be asked for permission from www.tailwind.zone to see your account address.
    <img 
      className="block"
      src="/images/permissions.png"
      alt="Permission"
    />
    Click "Approve" to use the Dashboard. Only approve permissions for sites you trust.
  </Step>

</Steps>
